1) Go to https://www.ecad.eu/dailydata/predefinedseries.php and download:
- "Daily snow depth SD" - ECA_blend_sd.zip
- "Daily mean temperature TG" - ECA_blend_tg.zip
- "Daily precipitation amount RR" - ECA_blend_rr.zip

2) Unzip the files if necessary and copy:
- 01_snow_depth.Rmd into ECA_blend_sd
- 02_temperature.Rmd into ECA_blend_tg
- 03_precipitation.Rmd into ECA_blend_rr

3) Run all 4 Rmd Notebooks => The resulting cdk1 dataframe contains blended daily snow depth values in cm for all countries in Europe
